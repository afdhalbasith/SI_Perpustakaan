library information

The vision of Perpustakaan XYZ are being an academic partner for the learning society based on the caring attitude and the learning spirit, giving inovative services, being oriented to users' satisfaction, being unlimited by space and time, and developing science and technology based on informational technology. The mission are being a professional partner for the learning society by acting as educational guides and researchers, being guides of teaching-learning processes with the concept of learning how to learn, being a central informational access for the global society in science which becomes a focus of the university (Library without walls).

Collections

We have many types of collections in our library, range from Fictions to Sciences Material, from printed material to digital collections such CD-ROM, CD, VCD and DVD. We also collect daily serials publications such as newspaper and also monthly serials such as magazines.


Library Membership

Dedicated to Informatics Engineering Students and Information System Study Program Students.If you are a new student and want to apply a membership

how an active Students License Card ( KTM-Kartu Tanda Mahasiswa ) to RBTC officer and Fill the registration form that served by the officer and pay the tax :

Rp. 5.000 for a year membership. ( Reguler S1 )

Rp. 10.000 for a year membership.( Reguler S2 )

Rp. 10.000 for a year membership.( Outsider )
